OVERVIEW

ROLE

In my spare time, I created an extracurricular endeavor called Plant Seeker, an application that uses camera scans to detect plants. When a plant is identified, it offers extensive details such as planting guidelines, thorough descriptions, maintenance advice, and infection cures.

With its comprehensive approach to gardening, Plant Seeker seeks to provide both new and experienced gardeners with insightful advice on plant care and upkeep.
